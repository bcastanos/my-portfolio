About Me

As a developer with a philosophy degree, I am not only interested in the logical aspects of web development but also the creative opportunities that the field affords its practitioners. I’m recently finished a 12 week web development immersive at GA where I have been focusing on Node.js, AngularJS, MongoDB, Express.js, CSS, Javascript, and Ruby on Rails.

Most recently, I was a business owner who created a startup poke bowl business operating at farmer’s markets across the Los Angeles area. While this experience taught me many things, one of the most important was a strong work ethic as I engaged in 12 hour days that often started at 4am or earlier! Another thing the business taught me was process improvement. Neither my business partners nor myself had any experience with farmer’s markets, so each shift was an opportunity to learn what worked, and also to learn what changes needed to be made to improve the efficiency and appeal of our operation. I’m now focused on bringing that hard work and dedication to a growing start-up where I can utilize my dev tools to participate in building a better (and profitable) future.
